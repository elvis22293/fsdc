Structural Health Monitoring
for Civil Structures

FiberSensing Structural Health Monitoring systems and products based on Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) technology are designed to accurately determine structural parameters of a civil structure, such as bridges, dams, tunnels, historical buildings…, during its construction, design validation and life time operation.

This technology is nowadays well established in the Civil Engineering market, in which FBG monitoring solutions have proven particularly cost-effective when applied to large scale structures, allowing the deployment of hundreds of sensors for long term measurement of different physical parameters.
