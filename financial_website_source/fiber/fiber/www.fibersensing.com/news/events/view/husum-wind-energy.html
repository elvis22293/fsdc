Husum Wind Energy

Events

For the last 25 years HUSUM Wind has been the leading biennial trade fair for the wind industry.

Visitors will have again the opportunity to learn about energy transition and wind power in action – with a special practical and market orientation – in an environment full of pioneering spirit and innovative power.

HBM FiberSensing will be exhibiting at the HBM stand to demonstrate the latest updates on WindMETER, the company's innovative optical blade monitoring system (15-18 September).

Husum, Germany
